Urban Shifters Domestic Move

Urban Shifters Ltd. has devised convenient and brilliant ways to move its customers' household items or vehicles anywhere within the territory. Our services are designed proficiently to meet the maximum customer satisfaction, and they are also rendered in such a way that comes out high on the expectations of our clients. Being the leaders in Packing & Moving business, we are best known for offering an unparalleled, plethora of efficient moving services in the domestic sector. We ensure that deviations, of any form, do not creep in and hamper our efficiency. However, in case of an unfortunate accident, despite of all safety precautions undertaken, we try to take the entire responsibility for the loss thence incurred and compensate fairly.

Our wide assortment of Household Moving Services includes:

  • Free On-site Estimate
  • Quality Packing Material
  • Full Service Packing, Transportation and Unpacking
  • Removal and Storage

The most important thought in your mind that worries you the most while shifting is the care and safety of your belongings having tremendous emotions attached to them.

We deeply understand your emotions attached to your belongings. To overcome this, we have developed the best logistics and transportation services as an ideal solution to all your shifting problems.

We, the best moving and packing company use international quality materials & vehicles to protect your goods from damage, moisture or dust. Our professionals have developed various ingenious solutions for all the shifting problems that may be encountered during the move.

We perceive certain distinctive precautions while loading the goods like items having religious significance are labeled and placed on top so that no one inadvertently steps on them.

While transporting goods, we ensure ‘0’ transshipment to prevent breakage. Our vehicles ensure that all your delicate and priceless belongings travel cocooned in total comfort and maximum safety to reach the destination in the perfect shape.

Safe Storage Options are also offered in our world-class warehouses as per your demand until your house is being ready to move in. We at APML, ensure that ‘Nothing other than the place changes…’ We shoulder all your moving worries and tensions by putting you at complete ease.

Everything is Handled With Extreme Care!

Being our valuable customer, you can be assured completely of availing the safest and fastest Domestic Moving Services, which are being executed at the right time, whereby ensuring that the goods get delivered to you in intact condition.

Our dedicated and committed workforce is well-versed with the entire packing and moving process. Packing materials like high-quality fabric sheets and corrugated sheets are used to provide complete safety to your precious household items and protection against any kind of breakage during transition.

That is what makes us one of the best removals and storage service providers in this sector nationally as well as internationally. We have a boastful clientele that makes us feel proud of our services and staff at the end of each passing day.
